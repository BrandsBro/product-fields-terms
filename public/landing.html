Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

How do I show fields on my product page?

Go to Online Store → Themes → Customize → Product page → Add block → find 'Product Options' under Apps section.

Where do I see customer responses?

Go to Orders → click any order → responses appear under each product as line item properties.

Can I assign fields to specific products?

Yes! Inside each field group, use the Product targeting section to assign to specific products or collections.

How do I add options to a dropdown field?

Open a field group → click on a Dropdown field → click Options → add your choices. You can drag to reorder.

Can I hide fields without deleting them?

Yes! Use the Deactivate button on any field group to hide it from your storefront without deleting it.

Is this app free?

Yes! Product Options and Terms is completely free during our beta period.
